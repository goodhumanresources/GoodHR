Get in Touch

Good HR works with companies across fintech, healthcare, education, and enterprise environments — from growth-stage startups building HR from the ground up to established mid-market organizations navigating transition, compliance gaps, or leadership change. If you have a people function that needs ownership, we're likely a fit.

Engagements are structured on an interim, project, or advisory basis depending on what the organization needs. Scope, timeline, and deliverables are defined upfront before work begins. Pricing is engagement-specific — retainer, project-based, or hourly depending on the nature of the work.

Good HR works both remotely and in-person. The right model depends on the engagement — some work requires physical presence, particularly for sensitive ER matters or organizational change. Most operational and advisory work can be handled fully remote. We discuss this during the initial scoping conversation.

A fractional engagement means Good HR operates as a part-time embedded HR leader — typically a defined number of hours per week or month — on an ongoing basis. An interim engagement is full-time (or near-full-time) coverage for a defined period, often during a leadership transition, leave, or organizational change. Both involve full ownership of the work.

For most engagements, Good HR can begin within 1–2 weeks of an executed agreement. For urgent matters — active ER situations, critical compliance gaps, leadership departures — timelines can be compressed. Reach out and we'll discuss what's feasible given your situation.

Core expertise includes Workday HCM and ADP Workforce Now for HRIS. Good HR also works across ServiceNow, Greenhouse, Jira, and most major ATS and HRIS platforms. For organizations evaluating new systems, Good HR can support vendor selection, requirements gathering, and implementation oversight regardless of platform.
